Brooklyn Beckham Shares Adorable Photo Of Him Keeping Girlfriend Chloe Grace Moretz Safe

Publish date: 2024-05-04

Brooklyn Beckham is no stranger to sharing his love for girlfriend Chloe Grace Moretz at every possible moment by posting up a storm of Instagram shots, but the talented young photographer's latest black-and-white photo of the two lovebirds and the accompanying message might be one of his sweetest yet.

"Keeping her safe," the 17-year-old captioned the shot, which shows the pair framed in a dark doorway with white doors on either side. This time the Neighbors actress plays photographer, snapping a mirror selfie as she is held up and supported in her boyfriend's arms in a very Notebook-esque pose. Embed:
