Career

MrBeast started his career as a YouTuber, his video generally features attention-grabbing stunts. He donated most of his income to individuals and even hosts events in games like Minecraft for many big prizes to help others. In one of his gaming videos, he donated a house to an individual.

In the year 2017, he uploaded a 40-hours long video on his YouTube channel, and in the very next month, he uploaded a 55-hours long video. In the year 2019, he organized a real-life battle royale competition in Los Angeles in collaboration with Apex Legends with prizes worth $200 thousand.

Most of his YouTube videos are sponsored by various companies like Honey and others. In the year 2021, he released a video titled Rewind 2020, Thank God It’s Over on his YouTube channel. In the year 2019, he co-created a fundraising event titled TeamTress on Youtube along with popular YouTubers and former NASA engineer Mark Rober.

Many popular YouTubers like Rhett & Link, Marshmello, The Slow Mo Guys, Ninja, and several others support the idea which mainly focuses on raising funds for Arbor Day Foundation and planting trees in national parks of the United States. His project also received huge donations from several corporate executives and companies.
