Who Has The Bachelorette Star Dated?

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Since wooing Hannah Brown on The Bachelorette, Tyler Cameron has been spotted with a slew of eligible ladies. Tyler reportedly dated Gigi Hadid for two months in 2019 before splitting up. Per People, the breakup was amicable. "It wasn't a bad breakup or anything like that," Tyler said in October 2019. "It was just, we're at different parts of our lives right now." 

Tyler definitely has a model type. He was linked to Elizabeth Turner in November 2020 before reportedly moving on with Fashion Nova star Ireland Borba, according to E!. "He keeps in touch with several ladies, but it's always casual. Tyler isn't looking for anything serious," a source stated. 

In January 2021, Tyler was spotted with a new woman, influencer Camila Kendra. Per Us Weekly, the pair drove from Florida to New York together. "Tyler and Camila both live in Florida so they have kept in touch and made plans to see each other these last couple of months," a source told E!. "They are both into each other but it's a stretch to say she's his girlfriend." 

As reported by Bustle, Tyler just brought Camila along to visit Bachelor Matt James in New York City and stay in Tyler and Matt's shared apartment. Camila definitely wasn't on Matt's season, so maybe there's another mystery woman in the mix? Ah, the joy of young love.
