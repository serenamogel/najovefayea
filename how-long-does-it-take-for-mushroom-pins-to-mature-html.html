What is the cause of pinning?

Fresh air exchange is the primary cause and the number one pinning trigger. The effect of fresh air exchange is the evaporation of moisture from the surface of the substrate. A correctly designed fruiting chamber will maintain the right degree of humidity if it is misted and then fanned on a regular basis as directed.
