Interesting Facts about Ford Fry

  • According to his Wikipedia information, he was born in the year 1969. He is 51 years old as of 2020. He celebrates his birthday on every 17th day of June.
  • Ford is a chef and restaurateur based on Atlanta Georgia.
  • He was born and raised in Texas. While talking about his academics, he attended the New England Culinary Institute in Vermont.
  • After his studies at Culinary Institute, he worked in a number of restaurants in states of the U.S like California, Georgia, Florida, and Colorado.
  • After working for many years, he decided to open his first restaurant, The JCT Kitchen and Bar in the first month of 2007.
  • He lives in Roswell, Georgia along with his wife. He also has two children and both are son.
  • Ford Fry along with his team is rich in restaurants. They have about 11 restaurants in Georgia, 3 restaurants in Huston, and one in Charlotte.
  • He was also the semifinalist for the consecutive five years from 2013 to 2017 in the James Beard Foundation Outstanding Restaurateur Award.
  • In the year 2013, he won the Georgia Restaurant Association’s seventh annual Crystal of Excellence (Grace) Award.
  • The details about his net worth are not available. However, we can definitely say that he has earned quite a fortune from his number of restaurants.
