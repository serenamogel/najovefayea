9 Chic Ways to Pair Skirts and Boots

Publish date: 2024-07-12

Some outfit combinations stand the test of time because they simply just work. Skirts and boots as a pairing is a tale as old as time; they’ve been seen on countless runways and are always spotted on celebrities. Skirts and boots fill holes in everyday wardrobe needs, from comfort to adding a touch of femme to everyday wear. Not only do they complement each other, but skirts in varying lengths work wonders in creating memorable and interesting outfits filled with personality. 

Whether you love an all-black leather skirt paired with a matching black Chelsea boot or prefer to wear funkier ensembles like sequined skirts and moon boots, we’ve rounded up some of our favorite ways to wear boots and skirts.

Pair Cargo and Combat

Show some leg—or a lot—with a thigh-high slit cargo skirt that sits just below your hip to showcase a pair of black combat-style boots, it’s a fun twist on a utilitarian style.

Mix Metals

There are no rules when it comes to fashion, and that extends to mixing functional snow weather boots with something festive and sparkly like this midi tinsel fitted skirt.

Kick Up Your (Cowboy) Boots

The western-inspired shoe is getting tons of attention this season, including being seen on Bey herself on repeat. Cowboy boots are far more versatile than you’d think- they even work with pleated mini skirts like this one here.

Style Leather With Latex

A fitted latex midi skirt feels edgy when paired with a Chelsea leather bootie. Opt for a high-waisted latex skirt option with a slight in the front that ends right above the ankle for prime boot display.

Try the Unexpected

Your Ugg boots clean up nicely. Add them to a boho-inspired white flare skirt and white tube socks to give a cool street style vibe to even the daintiest and most ladylike look.

Switch Things Up

Get creative and layer a mini skirt over your go-to classic white button-down for some added dimension and to rock a high-thigh boot in any color for a simple, but cool look. 

Keep It Consistent

If you have a skirt that is the same color as a pair of boots, why not wear them together? Even better if the boot has added interest, like this funky novelty pair with a pop of neon green at the base.

Be Transparent

See-through skirts are an amazing opportunity to show off a favorite pair of leather cowboy boots. Dare to bare with a sheer tulle overlay skirt and slouchy black leather boots for a look that creates both major elegance and drama.

Get the Blues

Sometimes, having the blues can be a good thing. Try mixing and matching your shoes, handbag, and even your skirt to be in the same family blue. Being monochromatic makes for a no-brainer outfit that feels polished no matter the occasion.
