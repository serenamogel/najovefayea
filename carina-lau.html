Biography

Carina Lau was born in China on December 8, 1965. Chinese actress and singer who’s notably known for playing Wu Zetian in the blockbusters Detective Dee and the Mystery of the Phantom Flame, Flying Swords of Dragon Gate and Young Detective Dee: Rise of the Sea Dragon. She won the Best Actress Award at the 2014 Osaka Asian Film Festival for her starring role as Anna in the 2013 film Bends with Chen Kun. Her first TV role was Fong Yee in the 1984 TV series The Duke of Mount Deer. She rose to fame after playing Sandy Ngai Chor Gwun in Looking Back in Anger in 1986.
