My Project

My students are absolutely amazing! After a rough last year due to covid, my students are really working hard to catch up and be productive in school. Currently, we have a lot of old desks in the classroom and are in need of new ones. We're looking for desks that are sturdy, but that does not have a compartment for them to shove things into. The desks we have are so old, ugly, and have scratches all over them. Our current desks are also falling apart and are on the smaller side for my 6th graders. We would love some new desks for our classroom!
