Santiago Gangotena Muerte: Accidente Video

Publish date: 2024-02-07

Santiago Gangotena Muerte video is all over social media as the founder of USFQ was run over on April 21, 2023, by a bus. Here is everything you should know about his accidente. 

Santiago Gangotena was the founder of USFQ. Further, USFQ is the first totally private university in Ecuador. He was also the co-founder of the Colegio Menor San Francisco de Quito.

Santiago was also a writer and wrote many articles on science and education. Similarly, Santiago traveled to the United States to pursue his career and received a B.S. in physics and a Master’s in nuclear physics.

Gangotena was well educated, and as per the sources, he received Ph.D. in Nuclear Magnetic Resonance from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

Additionally, Santiago is regarded as a prolific writer and a columnist for the newspaper El Comercio and Hoy. While exploring his writing career details, he wrote books including The Fourth State of Mind, Meditation, and Logbook of Vegetarian Cuisine.

Gangotena was also the co-author of the third translation of Tao te Ching directly from Chinese to Spanish.

Santiago Gangotena Muerte (Death)

Santiago Gangotena died on April 21, 2023. He was run over by a bus on María Angélica Idrovo Street, a few meters from the Cumbayá roundabout.

The video of Santiago’s Muerte is going viral as it was caught on a CCTV camera. The Police department has already released the video, and everyone is mourning the death of Gangotena. 

Exploring his appearance, Santiago seemed to be in his 50s at his tragic death. Santiago was admired for his work and was close to many people. 

So, his sudden death news has left everyone sad, and they are paying tribute to Gangotena. The Gangotena family is also getting condolence messages via various social media platforms.

How Did Santiago Gangotena Die? Cause Of Death

Santiago Gangotena died on April 21, 2023, following a road accident. When he was hit by a bus, a security guard called 911, but the ambulance arrived between 15 and 20 minutes. 

Reportedly the ambulance went to opposite lanes, and at that time, Santiago died. He was rushed to the Hospital de los Valles by paramedics.
