How To Make Taco Tater Tot Casserole Recipe

This recipe takes about 15 minutes to prepare which is usually just long enough for my oven to preheat. Make sure to check out my Variations To Try section below for fun ways to customize this taco tater tot casserole.

  • Brown the ground beef until cooked through. Remove beef from skillet and drain any excess grease.
  • Add the onions and bell peppers to the pan, sauteon medium high heat for 2-3 minutes, until vegetables are softened.
  • Transfer the ground beef back to the pan, along with the salsa, green chiles, taco seasoning, salt and pepper.
  • Add the softened cream cheese and stir until well combined.
  • Spread the meat mixture evenly into the bottom of a casserole dish.
  • Spread the shredded cheese evenly across the top.
  • Line tater tots in rows on top of the cheese until completely covered.
  • Bake in the oven at 375 degrees for 20-25 minutes, or until tater tots are cooked through and slightly crispy on top. Top with the sliced green onions. Serve hot as is or garnish with suggested toppings (see below).

    FAQs

    Can this recipe be made ahead of time? You can make this dish ahead by preparing the meat mixture and refrigerating. Increase the cook time to 30-35 minutes since the meat will be cold.

    Can this recipe be frozen? Yes. Prepare the casserole without the tater tots. Wrap in plastic wrap and then tightly in foil. Freeze for up to two months. Let defrost in fridge overnight. Add tater tots and bake as directed.
