Dolly Parton has been begging Jolene not to take her man since the song was released in late 1973. At that time, the single quickly became a chart-topper, reaching #1 in both the United States and Canada. And now that Beyoncé has released her own cover of the song, it seems to be making a comeback (but let’s be real, the track never really lost steam). Nevertheless, the internet is having a field day with ‘Jolene'-related jokes and comments.

Jolene

Beyoncé’s highly anticipated album, Act II: Cowboy Carter, has finally been released and features the singer’s own version of the popular song with slightly altered harmonies and changed lyrics. For example, instead of the original’s “Your beauty is beyond compare/With flaming locks of auburn hair,” Beyoncé sang, “You’re beautiful beyond compare/Takes more than beauty and seductive stars.” Parton is in favor of the latest cover, tweeting, “Wow, I just heard Jolene. Beyoncé is giving that girl some trouble, and she deserves it!” Stars like Reba McEntire, Miley Cyrus, Kelly Clarkson, Olivia Newton-John, and Lil Nas X have all taken the mic to perform their rendition of the hit.
